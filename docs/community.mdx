# Hasura Community

## Events

Join us at our [events](https://hasura.io/events/) to learn more about Hasura and GraphQL.

## Discord

Join us on our [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/hasura) server to chat with the Hasura team and other community
members.

## Reddit

Join us on our [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/Hasura/) community to chat, hang out and share your Hasura projects.

## Stack Overflow

Ask questions on [Stack Overflow](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/hasura) using the `hasura` tag.

## LinkedIn

Follow us on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/hasura/) to stay up to date with the latest news and updates
from the company.

## Newsletter

You can subscribe to our newsletter and see all other community information on our
[community page](https://hasura.io/community/).
